About Conya Doss

Conya Doss, often hailed as the "Queen of Indie Soul," has been captivating audiences with her soulful vocals and heartfelt lyrics since the early 2000s. Her musical journey began with the release of her first single "Coffee" in 2002, which was featured on her acclaimed debut album "A Poem About Ms. Doss."

Drawing comparisons to neo-soul luminaries like Jill Scott, Erykah Badu, and India.Arie, Conya has carved out her own distinctive space in the music world. Her authentic approach to songwriting and performing resonates with listeners seeking genuine musical expression in an industry often driven by commercial trends.
Throughout her career, Conya has remained fiercely independent, releasing her music on her own terms and maintaining artistic integrity at every turn. This commitment to her craft has earned her a dedicated following and respect throughout the neo-soul community. With her warm tone, impeccable phrasing, and lyrics that speak to the heart, Conya Doss continues to create music that moves the soul and stands the test of time.
